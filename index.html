    Project Ideas

    Overview

    An interesting concept for an IT project would be to create a visual HUD with augmented reality for automotive vehicles. The hub will provide essential details to the driver to aid in travel. This should lower the chances of accidents and allow the driver to maintain focus on the road. This project is based off the similar system that fighter pilots have in their helmets. Our HUD will be an interactive instrument with both voice and touch input.

    Motivation

    I believe that this would be a useful and interesting project to complete as it brings science-fiction into the real world. Augmented Reality is in its early stages. As this technology progresses it will bring about a large change in how we do many things. From driving to mobile devices.
    Another added advantage would be to help drivers maintain concentration on the road. This can be done with the project as it allows the driver to see necessary information such as speed or GPS. This mean that the driver wouldn’t have to have their eyes wonder off the road.

    Description

    This electronic hub would be a film placed on the internal side of the front windscreen. This film would need to be ultra-thin and transparent in order not to affect the drivers view. This film would need a connection point and act as if it was a see-through smart phone screen. The screen is a direct display that will mainly mimic the cars dashboard and will contain the following features: Speedometer, GPS, Rear and Side View Mirrors, Fuel Gauge, Oil Temperature Gauge, Tachometer, Odometer, Location information and Distance Delta.
    The speedometer will be positioned in the lower middle of the drivers view point and, by default, it will be displayed in digital format instead of analogue. However, there will be an option for either, or both at the same time. The digital format will be placed in front of the analogue format.
    Secondly an onscreen GPS. The 2 main features are a map, which acts similar to google maps, and visual cues, to know which lane one should be in or other information. The map feature is similar to the basic satnav-like map which has two viewing modes, birds-eye and directional. Birds-eye is a top down view of the map whereas directional is angled to face the drivers forward direction. The visual cues consist of but are not limited to the following: directions, distance to directions, and Lane Selection.
    The third application is mirroring the rear and side view mirrors. This application will allow the driver to see each mirror on the HUD. The mirrors are displayed on the hub respective to their original position.
    Fuel gauge and oil temperature will be displayed in the lower right of the HUD. The fuel gauge will be displayed as a vertical bar with general design of F(full) being at the top and E(empty) being at the bottom and, 1/4-point, 1/10-point and, 1/20-point mark without text. The quarter point marked spots will be a larger indication than the 1/10-point marks. The 1/20-point marks will be the smaller indicators. The oil temperature gauge will be displayed in both numerical and analogue. The numerical display will be placed in a square to the right of the analogue display. This gauge will be displayed to the right of the fuel-gauge.
    On the HUD the tachometer will have 4 display types. Hidden, numerical, analogue and, horizontal bar. The hidden display will hide the tachometer from the driver. Numerical display will provide the tachometer data in a numerical fashion. The analogue display will be displayed similar to the tachometer in the driver’s car. The horizontal bar display is a colour-coded horizontal bar-graph red being redline RPM. Green and Yellow would be dependent on the engine of the car. This display can either be left or right of speedometer.
    The odometer is a device that shows the distance travelled by an automotive vehicle. This will be displayed in the lower left of the HUD.
    Location information and Distance Delta are to provide specific information to the driver. Location information will display speed limits of the roads and, possible points of interest. Speed limit displays allows the driver to go on unknown roads without the hassle of searching for a speed sign. Distance Delta is a distance function system which will display the distance from objects in the 4 points: forward, rear, right and, left. Distance delta would be displayed on both the mirrors and the lower section of the main HUD above the speedometer.
